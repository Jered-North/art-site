Star Guardian Ulario

I... Put in a LOT of effort for this one, but I wanted to try something new. A friend had this scrapbook paper that was all stars themed and I've been having a hankering to get my grubby fingers on it and use it for something. Also, I was able to pick up a lot of scrap mat board at a local framing place and was anxious to use that too. So I drew up the lion on bristol, colored it with colored pencil and copic marker, cut it out, glued it to the scrapbook paper (which I added acrylics and glitter glue to) and then painted the mat board with blue and white blurry clouds and spattered it with stars and glitter glue. Then I glued it all together!!

Anyways, I hope you enjoy!
