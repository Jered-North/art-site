The Mercenary of the Four Kingdoms

The mercenary- infamous across every kingdom due to the skill and precision he uses when he takes on and finishes his contracts. He is a master swordsman and speaks with a noble's tongue- yet treats everyone as old friends. Strangely, very few have seen his face- he often wears a bird-shaped mask, a cowl, a hood. But they say when he laughs, he caws like a crow.

He goes by the name Dynell. And the creatures of the enchanted forest know he is a Tengu-- a creature from a distant continent. There is no creature like him in this continent. They say... He ran from something. With skills like his, it's hard to imagine what.

Done with copic markers and colored pencils
