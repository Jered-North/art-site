Daughter of the Violets

I've been working with concepts for her for a little while, but I was writing an essay on Art nouveau and the Golden Age of Illustration and really wanted to do an Art Nouveau piece based on Alphonse Mucha's style. It took a LOT of work, especially since my larger nib pens stopped working. I did this all with a 0.3 brown copic multiliner and smaller, and it took me a few days to finish up. Plus I had to press harder because this is all done on illustration board which is THICK, THICK stuff.
